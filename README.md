# KeenKeeper 🌿

 Your personal shelf of meaningful connections. Browse, tend, and nurture the relationships that matter most.

---

## 📖 Short Description
**KeenKeeper** is a relationship management tool designed to help you stay intentional with your social circle. In a world of digital noise, KeenKeeper acts as a dedicated space to track your interactions, set contact goals, and ensure no meaningful relationship falls through the cracks. Whether it's a quick text, a phone call, or a video catch-up, KeenKeeper keeps your social health on track.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

*   **React.js** - UI components and application logic
*   **Tailwind CSS** - Modern, responsive styling and layout
*   **Recharts** - Dynamic data visualization for interaction stats
*   **React Router** - Seamless navigation between friends and details
*   **Lucide React / React Icons** - Clean, consistent iconography
*   **Date-fns** - Precise date formatting and manipulation

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Global State Management with Context API
KeenKeeper utilizes the **React Context API** to maintain a "Single Source of Truth." This ensures that your friend data and interaction history are synchronized instantly across the entire application—from the dashboard stats to the individual timeline cards—without the need for complex prop drilling.

### 2. Intelligent Interaction Timeline & Filtering
Keep a detailed log of every connection. The **Filtered Timeline section** allows you to navigate through your history effortlessly. You can view all interactions at once or drill down into specific categories like **Calls**, **Texts**, or **Video Chats** to see exactly how you've been spending your time with friends.

### 3. Visual Insight Analytics (Recharts)
Understand your social habits at a glance. Our **Stats section** features a beautifully designed doughnut chart powered by **Recharts**. It visualizes your interaction distribution, helping you identify if you're relying too much on texts or if it's time for more face-to-face video calls to keep those connections strong.
